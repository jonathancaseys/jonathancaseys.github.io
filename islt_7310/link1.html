Let's Learn about Sound: Vibration


When we talk about sound vibrations, its usually something that we feel or hear. It is not often something that we can see. Whether its the strike of a snare drum or the strum of the strings of a guitar, usually what we can see is blurry movement, but not individual vibrations. In the clip to the left, the band Metallica teams up with a video crew to record slow motion video of their perfomance, including drums, bass, piano, and guitar.

Here, the video crew is using close-up footage using high-speed cameras with an extrememly high framerate to capture individual vibrations that we can now see with the naked eye!

In the second clip, I have created some of these same effects using a smartphone and some simple instruments. I first use my camera to record a tuning fork being dipped into water, and then I record my ukulele strings being strummed.
